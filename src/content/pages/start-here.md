# A digital handbook for living a more mindful life

Below is a guide on how to get the most out of everything you'll find on this website.

## Intention

My intention for this website was to build a comprehensive library of ideas, information, tools and resources to help you live a more mindful life. You can think of it like a digital handbook.

I chose to ignore the rules of how information "should" be on a website and share information in the format that makes most sense to a _human_.

This means sometimes a post will be a bitesize chunk of information, a thought provoking idea, a step-by-step guide or even a simple list of bullet points.

## Inspiration

It's inspired and influenced by the philosophy of 'digital gardens' where posts aren't necessarily organised by their date and links are intentionally sprinkled throughout to guide you to different parts of the website.

It's designed to be an exploration of curiosity.

## Collaboration

It's also designed to be collaborative — I want to know what gets sparked in you when you read and explore this collection of ideas. At the bottom of every post you'll find a comment area. Please share your thoughts. I love to hear them!

## Areas

You can explore this website through each area of focus:

- Life design
- Digital wellness
- Analog systems
- Digital systems
- Habits and routines
- Mindful action

## Types of post

- #Seed — bitesize posts of quick wins, ideas or notes
- #Sprout — a little more in depth on a topic or idea
- #Tree — complete guides and in-depth resources you can follow step-by-step
- #Root — emerge over time and will be the philosophies, principles and practices that lay the foundation to living a more mindful life
