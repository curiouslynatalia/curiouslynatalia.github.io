> Who am I?

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/68ffe9176e8683326214ec5a/a313b49f-50c4-4ef9-a4b1-cd7ead5c8680/Natalia+%E2%80%93+More+Mindful+Life.JPG)

I’ve been obsessed with living life on my own terms since I was young. That might sound like I just never grew out of the rebellious teenager phase but quite the opposite is true.

I spent most of my younger years trying to fit in only to find myself feeling deeply lost every time. But after years of self-inquiry, asking big [questions](https://moremindfullife.com/blog/ask-better-questions-and-more-of-them) and intentionally designing my life, I can honestly say I now live a life true to myself.

If you’re curious to know, here’s what I’m up to [now](https://moremindfullife.com/now).

When I’m not thinking deeply about life or writing my [Better Than Reels newsletter](https://betterthanreels.substack.com/), I’m mindfully designing websites for holistic practitioners and creatives at [For the Do—Gooders](https://forthedogooders.com/).
